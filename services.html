WHAT WE DO

At Watcher Tattoo, we specialize in creating bold, unique, and lifelike tattoo designs. From black & grey and colour tattoos to portraits and cover-ups, our expert artists bring your vision to life with precision and creativity

Portraits

Our portrait tattoos in Glasgow are crafted with exceptional detail and precision, capturing lifelike expressions and emotions. Specializing in realism tattoo artistry, our experienced artists transform photographs of loved ones, icons, or personal inspirations into timeless works of body art. Using advanced shading techniques and premium inks, we ensure every portrait tattoo is both striking and long-lasting. Perfect for those seeking a meaningful, custom piece that tells a personal story, our portrait tattoos are designed to be as unique as you are.

Cover Ups/Rework

Our tattoo cover-ups in Glasgow are designed to transform old or unwanted tattoos into stunning new pieces you’ll love. Whether it’s faded ink, a design you’ve outgrown, or a tattoo that no longer reflects your style, our experienced artists specialize in creating custom cover-up tattoos that completely reimagine your skin with fresh artistry. Using advanced shading, colour realism, and precision detailing, we ensure your new tattoo not only hides the old one but becomes a piece of art you’re proud to show off. Every cover-up is tailored to your vision, making it a flawless second chance at body art.
