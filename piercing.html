Is piercing painful?

The level of pain varies depending on the individual's pain threshold and the location of the piercing. Most piercings are quick and cause only a momentary sharp pinch followed by a dull ache. Our piercers use techniques to minimize discomfort.

How do I care for my new tattoo?

Proper aftercare is crucial for healing. We recommend keeping your new tattoo clean with a mild, unscented soap and moisturizing it regularly with a thin layer of recommended lotion. Avoid direct sunlight and soaking in water during the healing process. Detailed instructions will be provided after your session.

What is the age requirement for tattoos and piercings?

For tattoos, you must be at least 18 years old with a valid government-issued ID. For piercings, minors aged 16-17 may get certain piercings with parental consent and the presence of a legal guardian, along with required identification for both parties.
