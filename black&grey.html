FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONs

Common questions clients ask about getting Black & Grey tattoos at our studio.

Why should I choose Black & Grey over Colour tattoos?

Black & Grey tattoos are timeless, age beautifully, and often require less touch-up compared to colour tattoos. They’re also great for achieving realism, portraits, and dramatic shading effects.

How long does a Black & Grey tattoo last?

Black & Grey tattoos tend to hold up better over time than coloured tattoos because black ink fades more slowly. With proper care, they can look sharp and detailed for decades.

Do Black & Grey tattoos hurt less than Colour tattoos?

Pain levels are similar, but many clients find Black & Grey tattoos more tolerable because they often require less time in one sitting compared to full-colour pieces.

What designs work best in Black & Grey?

Portraits, realistic imagery, religious themes, nature, and dramatic shading work especially well in Black & Grey. The style is versatile and can suit anything from small pieces to full sleeves.

How should I care for my Black & Grey tattoo?

Follow standard tattoo aftercare: keep it clean, apply a thin layer of recommended ointment, and avoid direct sunlight and swimming until healed. Proper aftercare ensures your Black & Grey tattoo stays crisp and detailed.
