Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 August 2025

At Watcher Tattoo, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as name, email, phone number, and address when you fill forms or contact us.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device information when you use our website.
  • Usage data, including pages visited, time spent, and interactions with our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To improve our website and services based on user behavior.
  • To send promotional content if you opted in for newsletters.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, track usage, and provide personalized content. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers to perform website analytics or marketing, subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the details below.
