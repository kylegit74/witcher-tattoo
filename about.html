From Passion to Tattoo Mastery

Angel’s journey into tattooing began at the age of 15, driven by a deep passion for art and self-expression. Despite a lack of local mentors or apprenticeship opportunities, he was determined to learn. He remembers building a homemade tattoo machine from an old radio motor and a guitar string, testing it out on fruit and pig skin.

In 2010, after saving up for a professional tattoo kit, Angel tattooed his first person—his mother, who has been a strong supporter from day one. She often offered her own skin so Angel could practice, helping him build confidence and skill until the machine felt as natural in his hand as a pencil.

Mitov quickly made a name for himself, winning multiple awards at the Bulgarian Tattoo Show over several consecutive years.
