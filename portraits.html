FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers to common questions about portrait tattoos and how we create them with precision.

Can any photo be turned into a tattoo?

Most clear, high-resolution photos can be used. Our artists may adjust details to ensure the tattoo translates well onto skin.

Are portrait tattoos only black & grey?

No — portraits can be done in both black & grey or full color. Each style offers a different mood and artistic impact, and we’ll help you choose what fits best.

How realistic will my tattoo look?

Our artists specialize in realism. With careful shading, highlights, and fine line work, the tattoo will closely resemble the subject of your photo.

Do portrait tattoos fade over time?

Like all tattoos, portraits may soften with age. Proper aftercare, sun protection, and occasional touch-ups help keep them looking sharp and lifelike for years.
